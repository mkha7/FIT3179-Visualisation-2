Malaysian Property Market

This visualization provides an overview of property transactions across Malaysia, allowing you to explore the differences between East and West Malaysia, and their transaction volumes and values.
This chart presents the House Price Index (HPI) across different states in Malaysia for 2023, highlighting the highest and lowest indices in Kuala Lumpur and Kelantan, respectively.
This scatterplot shows the relationship between income-to-price ratios and median property prices across Malaysian states, offering insights into the affordability of properties in each region.
The bar graph displays the median rental prices and yields for different Malaysian states, providing insights into which states offer the highest rental yields and prices in 2023.
This scatterplot compares sales performance and vacancy rates across Malaysian states, also indicating the number of new property launches in each region.
